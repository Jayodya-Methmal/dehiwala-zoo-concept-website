Animal Demostrations

Dolphins are known for their intelligence. They communicate with each other using a language made up of a series of noises and ultrasonic pulses released directly by their larynx. That's why the dolphin show at Dehiwala Zoo is so entertaining; you can witness their communication skills as well as how they interact with other dolphins.

The show with our sea lions is a must-see for visitors to Dehiwala Zoo. They are so nice that they feed people and children from their hands, making us laugh and thrilling us with their sweetness and intellect. Because the sea lion display is held every day of the week at different times
